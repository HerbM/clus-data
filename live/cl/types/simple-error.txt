====== Condition Type SIMPLE-ERROR ======

====Class Precedence List====
**simple-error**, **[[CL:Types:simple-condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:error]]**, **[[CL:Types:serious-condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:t]]**

====Description====
The type **simple-error** consists of //[[CL:Glossary:condition|conditions]]// that are signaled by **[[CL:Functions:error]]** or **[[CL:Functions:cerror]]** when a //[[CL:Glossary:format control]]// is supplied as the function's first argument.
