====== Constant Variable ARRAY-RANK-LIMIT ======
The upper exclusive bound on the //[[CL:Glossary:rank]]// of an //[[CL:Glossary:array]]//.

====Constant Value====
A positive //[[CL:Glossary:fixnum]]//, the exact magnitude of which is //[[CL:Glossary:implementation-dependent]]//, but which is not less than ''8''.
