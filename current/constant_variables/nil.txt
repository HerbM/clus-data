====== Constant Variable NIL ======

====Constant Value====
**nil**.

====Description====
**nil** represents both //[[CL:Glossary:boolean]]// (and //[[CL:Glossary:generalized boolean]]//) //[[CL:Glossary:false]]// and the //[[CL:Glossary:empty list]]//.

====Examples==== 
<blockquote>
nil <r>NIL</r>
() <r>NIL</r>
'nil <r>NIL</r>
'() <r>NIL</r>
</blockquote>

====See Also====
  * **[[CL:Constant Variables:t]]**

====Notes====
None.

====Editor Notes====
The //[[CL:Glossary:symbol]]// **nil** is also sometimes used for other purposes as well. For example, as a //[[CL:Glossary:designator]]// (//e.g.// a //[[CL:Glossary:stream designator]]//).
