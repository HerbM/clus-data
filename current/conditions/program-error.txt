====== Condition Type PROGRAM-ERROR ======

====Class Precedence List==== 
**program-error**, **[[CL:Types:error]]**, **[[CL:Types:serious-condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:t]]**

====Description====
The type **program-error** consists of error conditions related to incorrect program syntax. The errors that result from naming a //[[CL:Glossary:go tag]]// or a //[[CL:Glossary:block tag]]// that is not lexically apparent are of type **program-error**.
