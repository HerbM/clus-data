====== Constant Variable LAMBDA-LIST-KEYWORDS ======

====Constant Value====

a //[[CL:Glossary:list]]//, the //[[CL:Glossary:element|elements]]// of which are //[[CL:Glossary:implementation-dependent]]//, but which must contain at least the //[[CL:Glossary:symbols]]// \keyref{allow-other-keys}, \keyref{aux}, \keyref{body}, \keyref{environment}, \keyref{key}, \keyref{optional}, \keyref{rest}, and \keyref{whole}.

====Description====

A //[[CL:Glossary:list]]// of all the //[[CL:Glossary:lambda list keywords]]// used in the //[[CL:Glossary:implementation]]//, including the additional ones used only by //[[CL:Glossary:macro]]// definition //[[CL:Glossary:forms]]//.
