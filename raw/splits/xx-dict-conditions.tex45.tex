====== Restart USE-VALUE ======

====Data Arguments Required====

a value to use instead (once).

====Description====

\Therestart{use-value} is generally used by //[[CL:Glossary:handlers]]// trying to recover from errors of //[[CL:Glossary:types]]// such as **[[CL:Types:cell-error]]**, where the handler may wish to supply a replacement datum for one-time use.
