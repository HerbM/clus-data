====== Condition Type PACKAGE-ERROR ======

====Class Precedence List==== **[[CL:Types:package-error]]**, **[[CL:Types:error]]**, **[[CL:Types:serious-condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:condition]]**, **[[CL:Types:t]]**

====Description====

The type **[[CL:Types:package-error]]** consists of //[[CL:Glossary:error]]// //[[CL:Glossary:conditions]]// related to operations on //[[CL:Glossary:packages]]//. The offending //[[CL:Glossary:package]]// (or //[[CL:Glossary:package]]// //[[CL:Glossary:name]]//) is initialized by \theinitkeyarg{package} to **[[CL:Functions:make-condition]]**, and is //[[CL:Glossary:accessed]]// by the function **[[CL:Functions:package-error-package]]**.
