====== Accessor CHAR, SCHAR ======

====Syntax====

**{char}** //string index// → //character// **{schar}}** //string index// → //character// **{(**setf** (**Multi** //string index// → //new-character//** //string index//) //character//)

{char schar}

====Arguments and Values====

//string// - for **[[CL:Functions:char]]**, a //[[CL:Glossary:string]]//; for **[[CL:Functions:schar]]**, a //[[CL:Glossary:simple string]]//.

//index// - a //[[CL:Glossary:valid array index]]// for the //string//.

//character//, //new-character// - a //[[CL:Glossary:character]]//.

====Description====

**[[CL:Functions:char]]** and **[[CL:Functions:schar]]** //[[CL:Glossary:access]]// the //[[CL:Glossary:element]]// of //string// specified by //index//.

**[[CL:Functions:char]]** ignores //[[CL:Glossary:fill pointers]]// when //[[CL:Glossary:accessing]]// //[[CL:Glossary:element|elements]]//.

====Examples====

<blockquote> ([[CL:Macros:defparameter]] my-simple-string (make-string 6 :initial-element #\\A)) → "AAAAAA" (schar my-simple-string 4) → #\\A ([[CL:Macros:setf]] (schar my-simple-string 4) #\\B) → #\\B my-simple-string → "AAAABA" ([[CL:Macros:defparameter]] my-filled-string (make-array 6 :element-type 'character :fill-pointer 5 :initial-contents my-simple-string)) → "AAAAB" (char my-filled-string 4) → #\\B (char my-filled-string 5) → #\\A ([[CL:Macros:setf]] (char my-filled-string 3) #\\C) → #\\C ([[CL:Macros:setf]] (char my-filled-string 5) #\\D) → #\\D ([[CL:Macros:setf]] (fill-pointer my-filled-string) 6) → 6 my-filled-string → "AAACBD" </blockquote>
