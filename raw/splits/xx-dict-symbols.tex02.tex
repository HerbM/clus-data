====== Type KEYWORD ======

====Supertypes====

**[[CL:Types:keyword]]**, **[[CL:Types:symbol]]**, **[[CL:Types:t]]**

====Description====

The type **[[CL:Types:keyword]]** includes all //[[CL:Glossary:symbols]]// //[[CL:Glossary:interned]]// \thepackage{keyword}.

//[[CL:Glossary:Interning]]// a //[[CL:Glossary:symbol]]// in \thepackage{keyword} has three automatic effects:

\beginlist \item{1.} It causes the //[[CL:Glossary:symbol]]// to become //[[CL:Glossary:bound]]// to itself. \item{2.} It causes the //[[CL:Glossary:symbol]]// to become an //[[CL:Glossary:external symbol]]// of \thepackage{keyword}. \item{3.} It causes the //[[CL:Glossary:symbol]]// to become a //[[CL:Glossary:constant variable]]//. \endlist
