====== Variable *MODULES* ======

====Value Type====

a //[[CL:Glossary:list]]// of //[[CL:Glossary:strings]]//.

====Initial Value====

//[[CL:Glossary:implementation-dependent]]//.

====Description====

\Thevalueof{*modules*} is a list of names of the modules that have been loaded into the current //[[CL:Glossary:Lisp image]]//.

====Examples====

None.

====Affected By====

**[[CL:Functions:provide]]**

====See Also====

**[[CL:Functions:provide]]**, **[[CL:Functions:require]]**

====Notes====

The variable **[[CL:Variables:*modules*]]** is deprecated.
